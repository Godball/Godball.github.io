The Game of Gods

The Gods play with the fate of the world. Take action and ensure mankinds survival in this epic conflict!

Fast-paced, real-time 1v1 action set in a grand mythological atmosphere!

Take the opponent by storm using magical spells and powerful abilities!

Gust of wind

Ativated by swiping on the ball. This works as a skill shot with fairly short cooldown, pushing the ball in its direction.

Mighty Olympic Wall

Stop the opponent from scroring by invoking a temporary wall spell!

Atomize

Temporarily shrink the ball to a much smaller size on click

Electrocute

Send out an electric shock to your opponent, causing all of his spells to go on cooldown for a short amount of time!
